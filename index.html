FAQ

Common Questions

What types of videos do you edit?
+
I edit anime videos, gaming content, documentary-style videos, commentary, comedy/funny videos, YouTube Shorts, and long-form YouTube content. I also design thumbnails and create motion graphics.
What's your turnaround time?
+
Typically 24 hours for Shorts and 2–4 days for long-form content, depending on complexity. Rush delivery is available on request.
How many revisions do I get?
+
I include 2 free revision rounds with every project. I aim to get the first cut close to what you need so revisions are minimal.
What formats do you deliver in?
+
I deliver in any format you need — MP4 (H.264/H.265), MOV, or ProRes. I export optimized for YouTube, Instagram Reels, or wherever you're publishing.
Do you work with new creators?
+
Absolutely. Whether you have 100 or 100,000 subscribers, I bring the same quality and attention to every project.
Can you match a specific editing style?
+
Yes — just share reference videos and I'll analyze and replicate the pacing, color tone, and overall energy of that style.
